Frequently asked questions

Are your products truly authentic?

Yes, all our products are carefully sourced and handcrafted by local artisans in Myanmar. We take great pride in ensuring the authenticity and cultural significance of each item in our collection.

How can I be sure of the quality of your products?

Quality is our utmost priority. Our products undergo rigorous quality checks to ensure that they meet our high standards. We work closely with artisans to maintain the highest level of craftsmanship.

Do you offer customization options for your products?

Currently, we do not offer customization services. However, we are constantly expanding our product range, so stay tuned for updates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, as well as PayPal, for online payments.

How long does it take for my order to be shipped?

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. Shipping times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen during checkout.
